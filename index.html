ABOUT RAVI RAVINDRA

Ravi Ravindra, now over 80 years old, is a physicist and spiritual searcher, has a PHD in Physics form the University of Toronto, and is Post Doctorate fellow in Philosophy and Comparitive Religion. at the Columbia University.

In his spiritual search Ravi was introduced to J. Krishnamurti, Madame de Salzmann, Zen Master Roshi Kabori, and underwent a deep immersion in the mystical teachings of the Indian and Christian classical traditions. He is the author of several books on religion, science, mysticism, and spirituality.
